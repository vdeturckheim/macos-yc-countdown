# Privacy Policy

**YC X26 Demo Day — macOS Countdown App**

*Last updated: February 16, 2026*

## Overview

YC X26 Demo Day ("the App") is a macOS menu bar application that displays a countdown timer. This privacy policy describes how the App handles user data.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected, stored, or processed.
- **No analytics or tracking** tools are used.
- **No network requests** are made. The App operates entirely offline.
- **No third-party services** or SDKs are integrated.
- **No cookies or identifiers** are used.

## Local Storage

The App stores two user preferences (display mode and countdown format) locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data:

- Never leaves your device.
- Is not accessible to the developer or any third party.
- Can be removed by deleting the App.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, frameworks, or libraries beyond the standard Apple system frameworks (SwiftUI, AppKit, Foundation, and Combine).

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on this project's GitHub repository.
